Milestone

Achieving your goals one milestone at a time.

What is Milestone?

Milestone is a web application that empowers youth impacted by unstable housing to work with their case managers on designing and navigating a path towards stable housing, one milestone at a time.

With Milestone, youth and young adults can track their goals with their case managers and contact them regarding any questions or concerns they have within a centralized platform.

The Problem

Youth impacted by unstable housing are often in need of various resources, such as requiring bus passes to commute to a meeting with their case manager, and mostly contact their case managers via text messaging. Due to this situation, case managers become overloaded with the number of youth and other clients they work with.

These youth require support and must work alongside their case managers to achieve their goals, whether it is finding stable housing or finding employment. However, due to their situation of unstable housing, their phone numbers can frequently change, making it difficult for their case managers to contact them.
